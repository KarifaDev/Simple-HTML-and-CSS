Welcome to Flow yoga studio

We offer group yoga classes in our beautiful shala conveniently located at the heart of Edmonton.

Hang out at our Flow café before or after your yoga class.

Flow yoga stands for:

1. Community

We believe that yoga should be accessible to everybody, that's why we continously offer community yoga classes by donation.

2. Quality

All our teachers at the studio are certified and passionate about the yoga practice and sharing it with you.

3. Philosophy

Yoga is more than making shapes with our physical bodies. We welcome you to try our meditation and yoga philosophy classes as a compliment to your physical practice.
